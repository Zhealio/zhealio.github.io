Frequently Asked Question

Ask a Question

Here are some of the most frequently questions I get from potential clients.

Ask a Question

How much do you charge/quote?

It all depends on the complexity of the video editing you want. Feel free to ask what you need so I can give you a custom pricing/quote.

What is your turnover time/how long does it take to create a reel?

Again, it all depends on the complexity of the editing. But I do respect my clients timely deadline, so on average I can finish a reel within 1-3 days.

Can you do long-form content?

Yes! I can also do long-form content, and my rates are usually similar (if not cheaper) per rendered minute for a regular reel (>1 minute).

Do you provide any other services?

Other than video editing & content ideation, I can also do thumbnail design, graphics design and motion graphics.
